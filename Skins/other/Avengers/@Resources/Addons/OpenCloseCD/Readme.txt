A tool that allows you to open a CD tray.

Format of Parameters:

D=drive


ProcessRunning.exe "$D"

Example:

LeftMouseUpAction=["#SkinsPath#Tools!\OpenCloseCD\OpenCloseCD.exe" E:]

Command will open the E: drive then make a popup dialog to close the drive when your done.
